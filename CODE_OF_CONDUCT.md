# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Crabcraft contributors commit to a welcoming, harassment-free community. Be
respectful, assume good intent, critique ideas rather than people, and make room
for participants with different backgrounds and experience levels.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
doxxing, sexualized attention, sustained disruption, or publishing another
person's private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through their GitHub
profile. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or
permanently restrict participation when behavior harms the community. Reports
will be handled as confidentially as practical, with attention to the safety of
the reporter.

This policy applies in repository spaces and when someone is publicly
representing the project.
